BIO

Hi there. My name is Jesus, but you can call me “G-sus”. I believe we live in a wonderful moment in time that allows for the integration of art and technology. The tech-art wave has come to stay and, somehow, I ended up in the middle of it.

With a background in different skills from film, animation, design and coding, I’m glad it’s hard to pigeonhole myself. Looking beyond the craft, is the magic behind the theatrical film, the amazement of handling a new mobile gadget and the laughs shared in a virtual space. I love to make people wonder beyond the surface. And hopefully, make this journey a little more fun.
